Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

That Guy Insurance Firm LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our insurance services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and mailing address
  • Business Information: Company name, industry type, number of employees, annual revenue
  • Insurance Information: Policy details, coverage needs, claims history, vehicle information, driver information
  • Communication Preferences: Your preferred method of contact and marketing preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide insurance quotes and bind coverage
  • Process applications, endorsements, and policy changes
  • Communicate with you via phone, email, and text message
  • Send renewal reminders and policy updates
  • Process claims and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and underwriting requirements
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access

3. Text Messaging (SMS) Program

When you provide your phone number and opt-in to our SMS program, you consent to receive text messages from That Guy Insurance Firm LLC regarding:

  • Insurance quotes and pricing updates
  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Document requests and submission reminders
  • Policy renewal notifications
  • Claims status updates
  • Payment reminders

Message Frequency: You may receive 2-4 messages per week during active quote periods. Existing clients typically receive 1-2 messages per month regarding renewals and policy service.

Message and Data Rates: Standard carrier message and data rates may apply. Check with your wireless carrier for details.

Opt-Out: You can cancel SMS messages at any time by texting "STOP" to any message you receive from us. You will receive one confirmation message confirming your opt-out.

Help: For assistance, text "HELP" to any message or contact us at (760) 858-1333.

We use TextMagic and other A2P (Application-to-Person) messaging providers to deliver SMS messages. Your phone number is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Insurance Carriers: To obtain quotes, underwrite policies, and process claims (including The Hartford, CNA, Cover Whale, AmTrust, and others)
  • Service Providers: SMS delivery services (TextMagic), email marketing platforms, document management systems, and payment processors
  • Regulatory Authorities: State insurance departments, fraud prevention agencies, and law enforcement when required by law
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified of any such change)

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers with access controls
  • Regular security assessments
  • Employee training on data protection

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time
  • File a complaint with your state insurance department

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze website traffic

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or prominent website notice.
